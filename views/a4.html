Introduction

For this assignment, I have created two pieces of animated images. Animation has been a favorite hobby of mine and as I first started early 2017, I started off with Adobe Flash but it was discontinued not so long ago and due to university and my drive to learn about programming, I drifted away from creating animations. This assignment allowed me to reconnect with my oldself as I really loved working with animation softwares and transitioning from Adobe Flash to Adobe Animate wasn't a rough step up, I enjoyed the challenge and got used to it rather quickly and learned about newer features at the same time.
My two animated art works are as follows, one is of an anime character, Monkey D. Luffy and the other art work is of a water dispenser filling up an empty glass with water.

Monkey D. Luffy is the protagonist of the most popular and longest running anime "One Piece" and this GIF refers to his love for meat.
