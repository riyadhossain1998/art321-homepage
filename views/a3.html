Evil Memes

Internet memes has a been very popular type of net art ever since the early days of 4chan and reddit. Once memes became more reknown and people started becoming more involved in social media, it became more popular where some types have evolved, some had died out but never forgotten and some are still relevant till today. Example

However evil memes have are fairly new, inspired and possibly evolved from the humor of Deep Fried Memes but more straightforward and less abstract. I present to you, Evil Memes

You may skip this part if you understand how evil memes work but I would like to talk about it a little. Evil Memes are not really evil or true evil, the title might be a little misleading. In reality, the way to understand Evil memes is to be aware of the context the picture entails. For example, let us take this meme for example.

The colors in the image above has been inverted to portray the evil aspect of the context. Here the context speaks about the popular Linkin Park track In The End. The original lyric pertaining to the context is "In the end, it doesn't even matter". So, an evil version of Linkin Park would've actually said "In the end, it does matter."

To sum up, the evil meme consists of an inverted or highly saturated image of the context where the funny aspect of the meme is the opposite of the context.
