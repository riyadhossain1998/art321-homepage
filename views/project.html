Project: Expanding Johnny

Adobe Illustrator/Adobe XD

Overview

Expanding Johnny is a prototype of a sci-fi narrative choice based video game based on his adventures. A little insight about Johnny is that he is a normal human being working 9-5 on weekdays, comes home to play video games and a portal opens along with it came a man that looked just like him. I chose this as my project in order to improve content and creative writing skills, being able to create depth and engagement for viewers interested in the sci-fi field and try to think from outside the box. Here are some screens from the prototype.

Tools Used

I used Adobe XD to create this prototype and used Adobe Illustrator to create the characters and backgrounds of each of the scene.
